Array-based spectral opcodes

Note

These opcodes are designed to work with k-rate arrays for spectral data manipulation.

  • fft,
  • fftinv,
  • rfft,
  • rifft,
  • pvs2array,
  • pvsfromarray,
  • cmplxprod,
  • rect2pol,
  • pol2rect,
  • window,
  • r2c,
  • c2r,
  • mags, and
  • phs.
