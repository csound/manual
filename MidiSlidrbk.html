Slider Banks

Opcodes for slider banks of MIDI controls are:

  • slider8

  • slider8f

  • slider16

  • slider16f

  • slider32

  • slider32f

  • slider64

  • slider64f

  • s16b14

  • s32b14

  • sliderKawai

Opcodes for storing slider banks of MIDI controls to tables are:

  • slider8table

  • slider8tablef

  • slider16table

  • slider16tablef

  • slider32table

  • slider32tablef

  • slider64table

  • slider64tablef
