Comparators and Accumulators

The following opcodes perform comparisons between signals at a-rate or k-rate, find maxima or minima, or accumulate the results of several computations or comparisons:

  • max

  • max_k

  • maxabs

  • maxabsaccum

  • maxaccum

  • min

  • minabs

  • minabsaccum

  • minaccum

  • vincr

  • clear

  • cmp
