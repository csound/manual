Modifying FLTK Widget Appearance

The following opcodes modify FLTK widget appearance:

  • FLcolor

  • FLcolor2

  • FLhide

  • FLlabel

  • FLsetAlign

  • FLsetBox

  • FLsetColor

  • FLsetColor2

  • FLsetFont

  • FLsetPosition

  • FLsetSize

  • FLsetText

  • FLsetTextColor

  • FLsetTextSize

  • FLsetTextType

  • FLsetVal_i

  • FLsetVal

  • FLshow
