Signal Modifiers

Amplitude Modifiers and Dynamic processing

The opcodes that modify amplitude are:

  • balance

  • compress

  • clip

  • dam

  • gain

The opcode 0dbfs facilitates the use of amplitude by removing the need to use of explicit sample values.
